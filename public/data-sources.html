Data Sources

The start of any research or investigation is a source of data. Each row represents a source of public records about an agency in the criminal legal system. This database is always growing, thanks to community contributions.

At the top of the view below, you have tools for filtering, grouping, and sorting. By default, freshly approved sources are at the top. Try filtering by your state or county!

If you find an inaccuracy, please reach out to contact@pdap.io to correct the record.
