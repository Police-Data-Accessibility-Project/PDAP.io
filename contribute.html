How to contribute

Thank you for your interest in helping our mission!

We're a small organization, so a little goes a long way. We recently received a grant to hire two full-time staff, but the rest of our infrastructure is on a shoestring budget.

Give money

PDAP is a non-profit, so your donations may be tax deductible.

Give time

Collect data

If you can use a web browser, you can contribute to our mission. Start with the docs

Share your perspective

Have you ever downloaded criminal justice data? Do you work in local government? Do you see risks we should address?

Reach out to contact@pdap.io or join our Discord. We'll incorporate your feedback into our work.
