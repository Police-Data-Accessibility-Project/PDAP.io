How we use resources

Our time, money, and energy goes into centralizing public police data.

We spend money Data Bounties and lean infrastructure. Small recurring donations keep us stable, large donations help us make big leaps.

As an open source organization, we need a bare minimum of services to allow our core volunteer group to keep the lights on.

We can spend money on Data Bounties by financially rewarding contributors. Since we're paying for specific data, we can make larger and more focused improvements.
